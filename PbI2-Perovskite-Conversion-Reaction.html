Conversion Stoichiometry

Intercalation of MA+ and I- Ions
TSINGHUA UNIVERSITY
Stoichiometric Mechanism:
1. Precursor: Layered PbI2 (CdI2-type).
2. Intercalation: Both A-site cations and additional Iodides from the salt (e.g., MAI) enter the van der Waals gaps.
3. Reorganization: The extra Iodides bridge the [PbI6] units, converting edge-sharing layers into a 3D corner-sharing framework.
