Structural Transformation

From Layered PbI2 to 3D Perovskite
TSINGHUA UNIVERSITY
Phase Conversion Mechanism:
1. Initial State: Layered PbI2 (CdI2-type structure) with van der Waals gaps.
2. Intercalation: Organic cations (A-site) and iodides (I-ions) diffuse into the layers.
3. Reorganization: The edge-sharing [PbI6] octahedra transition to a 3D corner-sharing framework.
Lead (Pb2+) - 18 Units
