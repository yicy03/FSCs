Scientific Process:
1. Solution State: Randomly distributed ions are solvated by organic molecules (purple clusters).
2. Nucleation: As solvent evaporates, chemical potential drives ions to form coordinated [PbI₆]⁴⁻ octahedra.
3. Lattice Formation: Octahedra connect via corner-sharing, with A-site cations filling the voids.
